About

Halee Moss is a professional, certified bookkeeper and the founder of Rock Steady Bookkeeping. She’ll teach you all you need to know to minimise stress and inaccuracies and maximise insights into your business finances. All through using Xero accounting software in your small business.

Halee will come to you for personalised training in Christchurch, or educate you online via Skype throughout New Zealand. Personalised training ensures you receive the right information and advice for your level of understanding and business needs.

A common scenario is a small business owner who wants to take control of their finances with Xero:

Firstly, the business owner has a conversation with Halee so that she understands their needs and what the training must cover.

Secondly, Halee at Rock Steady will set-up the business in Xero or migrate the existing accounts over if this is needed.

Thirdly, the business owner upskills through an in-depth personalised two hour training session with Halee, at their premises or online. In this session they’ll gain the skills to manage Xero themselves. With the session Halee provides her ‘Rock Steady Xero Cheatsheet’ that the business owner can take away for later reference.

Finally, over the following weeks and months, Halee is available to help with specific questions and to provide more advanced training or services.

Services
